The Complete MCU Timeline

The Marvel Cinematic Universe timeline stretches from the ancient world to the far reaches of the multiverse. This guide presents the MCU in chronological story order, bringing together films and TV series into a single in-universe narrative. It can also serve as a chronological watch order for viewers who want to experience the story as it unfolds in the MCU timeline. Marvel has officially confirmed portions of the timeline through Disney+, which you can view on their official overview: Marvel’s official MCU timeline.

Some series connect to MCU events but are not currently included in Marvel Studios’ official timeline list; you can optionally include them below as “timeline-adjacent.”

Prefer release order? See the Movies and TV pages for full listings.
