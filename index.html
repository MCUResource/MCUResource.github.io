Canon Scope Statement

This site focuses on projects positioned as part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU), including theatrical films, television series, Marvel One-Shots, and Special Presentations. Projects set in alternate continuities that are not integrated into the MCU’s multiverse narrative are not included in primary listings, though they remain an important part of Marvel’s broader storytelling legacy.
