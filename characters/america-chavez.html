America Chavez

Full NameAmerica Chavez
AliasN/A
SpeciesHuman (from Utopian Parallel)
Portrayed ByXochitl Gomez
AffiliationMasters of the Mystic Arts
First MCU AppearanceDoctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022)
Skills & Abilities Multiversal travel via star-shaped portals, superhuman strength, enhanced durability, combat capability, dimensional awareness.

America Chavez is a multiverse-traveling hero with the rare ability to open star-shaped portals between realities. Her power makes her one of the most strategically important figures in the MCU’s expanding multiverse, capable of moving across timelines and dimensions in ways few others can.

When her abilities first manifested, America accidentally opened a portal and lost her family, leaving her alone and drifting between universes. This trauma shapes much of her early journey, as she struggles with fear, guilt, and a lack of control over her powers. Over time, she begins to understand that her strength comes not just from her abilities, but from trusting herself.

Determined to master her gifts, America begins training at Kamar-Taj, taking her first real steps toward control and confidence. With her multiversal mobility and growing self-belief, America Chavez stands poised to play a major role in the future of the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

MCU Appearances

  • Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness (2022)
